Cocktail is an HTML/CSS rendering engine written in Haxe,
used to build HTML/CSS/Haxe JS native applications and web apps.
This article is intended for Haxe developers.

A new Cocktail of Haxe magic

As announced in the presentation of Cocktail at the WWX2012, we have released the first beta of the Cocktail library on haxelib.

Yannick Dominguez and Rapahël Harmel will give a speech about this release during the WWX2013, register now for this great event.

What can you do with it ?

Recently, at Intermedia Paris, we have used Cocktail, along with the Brix framework to build the android version of the app of the french television channel TF1., presented in the official video.
