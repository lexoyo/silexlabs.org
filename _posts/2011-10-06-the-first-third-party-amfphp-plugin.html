We're very pleased to announce that amfPHP has it's first third party plugin, just weeks after releasing v2.0. It's called ServiceAccess, and it's by Bastien Aracil, at http://femtoparsec.net/ . You can find the plugin page here on the exchange platform. https://www.silexlabs.org/exchange/exchange-amfphp/serviceaccess-plugin/ If you too would like to share an amfPHP plugin with the community, you can also create a page here, so let us know!

From the post: ServiceAccess is a AMFPHP plugin to secured access to PHP services easily.
